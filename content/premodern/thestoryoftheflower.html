The Story of the Flowers

*The Story of the Flowers* shows how the Fire Flower and Ice Flower came to be.

*One day, long ago, before Mario and Luigi, a comet from space crashed on Earth. This comet was flaming, and it landed in a field with a bunch of flowers. Yoshis from a nearby village saw the comet in the sky. The chief gathered everyone at the big tree in the town square* Chief Yoshi: I need the four bravest Yoshi’s here. Any volunteers? *But no one raised their hand. Then one hand raised up, with three others following it. The four Yoshies then set off to find the comet* *They traveled through forests and mountains and were led by the non-stop smoke from the comet* Red Yoshi: Look, the smoke is getting closer! Yellow Yoshi: That means we’re almost there! *A few hours later, they had finally arrived at the comet. It landed in a field full of flowers.* Blue Yoshi: The flowers at the comet are different! Green Yoshi: Maybe we shouldn’t touch these until we know they’re safe… *Red Yoshi, ignoring the warning from Green Yoshi, touched the flower and visibly changed color* Red Yoshi: But look at this! I can shoot fire now! It is safe! Green Yoshi: Oh. Okay…let’s head back to the village and show the Chief *Just above Earth, two Lumas looked down. Luma 1: Looks like they found the fire comet. Ready to send the other one?” Luma 2: Let’s do it. *While the Yoshies were traveling back to the village, another comet hit the mountains nearby. Blue Yoshi: Another comet? We should go check up there! Green Yoshi: Let’s go, then! *At the mountain, there were more flowers, but they were blue rather than red. Green Yoshi: *to Blue Yoshi* So, what do these ones do?” said Green Yoshi to Blue Yoshi. “They do ice! I guess it makes sense since it’s colder up here Yellow Yoshi: Speaking of cold, let’s get out of here, because it’s freezing! *After the long journey, the Yoshis arrived back at the village, and went up to the chief hut. Chief Yoshi: These grant the user fire and ice powers? Green Yoshi: That’s right. Chief Yoshi: We’ll call them…Fire and Ice Flowers. *Just over one hundred years later, the Yoshis have passed down the flowers through generations. One day, someone showed up to Yoshi town. ???: I, Bowser, King of the Koopas, demand that you to give me all Fire and Ice Flowers, or else, your town will be destroyed! GRAHAHAHA! *The Yoshies didn’t want their town to be destroyed, so they handed them over, all but one Fire Flower and one Ice Flower. Once he had left, a Yoshi had Mail Koopa deliver the Fire and Ice Flowers* Mail Koopa: Delivery for Mario Mario: *while opening the box* What’s this? *In the box were two flowers and a note* Mario: ‘Yoshi Yoshi Yoshi…Yoshi…Yoshi?’ Luigi: I think it just says ‘Yoshi’. Mario: So, what if I touch the top of it? Luigi: Look, the blue on your shirt! It’s white now! And your hat’s white too! Mario: You try the blue one! Luigi: Are you sure it’s safe, though Mario: It’ll be fine! *Luigi uses the Ice Flower* And look! You’ve got a light blue shirt and hat now! Luigi: Oh, that’s kinda cool, actually! *glances at the box* Wait, there’s another paper here too! ‘Yoshi Yoshi Yoshi?’. What do you think that means? Mario: Maybe they want us to go get something? *running outside* Hey! Wait! Where’d the letter come from? Mail Koopa: The Yoshi Village.
